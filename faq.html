Frequently Asked Questions

What's the population of Fontana?

According to the 2019 U.S. Decennial Census, Fontana has a population of 214,547. Making it the 21st most populous city in California.

When was Fontana founded?

The city was founded by AB Miller(Azariel Blanchard Miller) in 1913.

What county does Fontana belong to?

Fontana was officially incorporated with the San Bernardino county in 1952.

Who is the mayor of Fontana?

Out of Compton, since 2010, the mayor of Fontana is Acquanetta Warren.

What's the average hourly rate in Fontana?
